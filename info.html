Projectiles

In 1600s, armies used equations of motions to calculate velocities and angle for firing a missle to hit a target. While a quarterback does not do explicit calculations using equations of motion, a computerized football game will certainly need to do these calculations.

We will use the metric notations that are facored for all scientific and engineering calculations

Abbreviations

The following abbreviations will be used:

  • meters, m
  • kilometers, km: 100 meters make up a kilometer
  • meters per second, m/s: units for measuring distance
  • meters per second squared, m/s 2: units for measuring acceleration

Notations

The following physics notation will be used:

Abbreviations Meaning
Initial velocity u
Final velocity v
Acceleration a
Time t
Distance s
